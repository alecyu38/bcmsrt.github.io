BC PPE Initiative

Personal Protective Equipment donations are needed for our frontline healthcare workers.

We need donations of closed packages of:

  • Surgical masks

  • N95 respirators

  • Eye protection

  • Protective gowns and gloves

  • Hand sanitizer

  • Medical-grade disinfectant wipes

During the COVID-19 crisis in British Columbia, there is an urgent need to maintain adequate personal protective equipment (PPE) for front-line health care workers at our hospitals. Over 40 students from various regions of BC (Vancouver, Victoria, Kelowna, Prince George and Chilliwack) are working together in this initiative. We have been collecting PPE donations from community donors (dentists, salons, small businesses, labs, industry, individuals) and distributing these donations across 9 hospital drop off sites that weâ€™ve helped establish across the province. We are also partnering with SafeCareBC Operation Protect.

If you have additional questions or are interested in participating in this initiative, contact regan_mah@alumni.ubc.ca or call 780-246-7890.

We have collected and distributed over 80,000 items of PPE including N95 masks, gloves, gowns and more across BC.
